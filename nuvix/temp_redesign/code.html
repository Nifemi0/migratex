Redefining institutional wealth management through an archival, brutalist digital identity.

01 / DISCOVERY

The Challenge

Lumina Capital approached Nuvixes with a critical tension: their legacy of conservative, high-stakes finance was clashing with a new generation of investors who demanded transparency, modernism, and digital agility. The existing brand felt cluttered, relying on dated financial metaphors and a lack of visual hierarchy.

The mandate was clear: strip away the noise. Create a digital experience that feels as stable as a stone monolith yet as precise as a Swiss watch.

The Solution

We implemented a "Curated Monolith" strategy. By utilizing a 12-column asymmetric grid and a strictly limited palette of Primary Navy and Off-White, we mirrored the permanence of archival documents.

TYPOGRAPHY

Custom treatment of Neue Montreal for dense, authoritative headlines and spacious metadata.

TONAL DEPTH

Replacement of borders with tonal stacking (#F4F3F1 on #FAF9F7) for a sophisticated feel.
